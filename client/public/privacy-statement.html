Information Collection and Use
This application collects only data relevant to login (so, your login name and credentials), your decks and gangs, and your scores. This data is not anonymised on the server but neither is it shared or used for any purpose other than your own and for those with whom you choose to share the data, meaning the members you invite to join your gangs, or the members of gangs that you join.
In the case of a social login, such as via Google, FlashGang accesses your user name, first name, last name, and avatar, for your exclusive use while using the application. This information is not shared with anyone with whom you haven't given explicit permission by participation in a FlashGang.


Log Data
There is no logging data, apart from anonymised system logs employed to monitor the health of the system.


Security
Your data is never transmitted to any third parties. Not for tracking purposes, not for marketing purposes, not at all.
